Meet
Kevin Zhang

I am an amateur photographer who is passionate about portrait photography. I love interacting with people and capture the best moments of their life. Always like to be taken part of the adventure.

I have completed my undergraduate study in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Toronto, currently a student at Rotman Master of Management Analytics. As a hobby, I have spent my time learning photography. I have other ongoing projects, including web/app design, data analytics projects and etc. Some of the projects are uploaded on my Github.

I love to travel with my camera to capture the beautiful landscape and culture.

Please feel free to inbox me with your photography request. I am open to being your photographer at any occasion, such as:

  • Professional
  • Graduation
  • Couple Anniversary
  • Other Portraits
  • Travel

In my leisure time, I learned how to play Ukelele, and I am further advancing my skills.
